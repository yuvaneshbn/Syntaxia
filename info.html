TECHNOPHITE

Department of Computer Science (PG)

TECHNOPHITE is an association founded by the Department of Computer Science (PG). It comprises of a team of passionate and organized youth who are interested in expanding their knowledge as well as their skills. The objective of the Technophite association is to widen the perspectives of Josephite's and to provide a platform for students to discuss, share and update their knowledge in various domains of information technology thereby encouraging them to undertake a multitude of curricular as well as extracurricular activities.

Vision

To actively organize and engage in various events that will update the students of the college with the plethora of technological advancements that transpire in ways that are fun and rewarding.

Mission

To engage and connect the postgraduate computer science students of our college and the ones across institutions all over India by active participation with in this community and to attend events, to volunteer, to learn, to grow and to contribute to the greatness of our institution.

Inter collegiate fest

An inter-collegiate fest "SYNTAXIA" is organized by the first and second year post graduate students. The fest included both on-stage as well as off-stage events that helped students to showcase their skills and their talents.
